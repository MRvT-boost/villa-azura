About Villa Azura

A Home Away From Home in Costa Blanca

Villa Azura is a charming, tastefully decorated holiday villa tucked away in the peaceful residential area of Los Almendros, just outside Benissa. The property offers the perfect balance of seclusion and convenience.

The spacious outdoor area features a large, deep private pool reached by a gentle Roman-style staircase, surrounded by palm trees and private terraces — ideal for sunbathing in the morning or relaxing under the stars at night with atmospheric garden lighting.

Indoors, a bright open-plan kitchen (fully renovated in 2025) connects to a welcoming living room and a generous covered terrace with an outdoor bar and BBQ. Air-conditioning throughout, free high-speed fibre WiFi, Netflix and Dutch-language satellite TV make it comfortable for any season.

The villa is fully fenced with a gated entrance and a security camera. A pool fence ensures young children stay safe. Two bedrooms have direct access to the pool area.

🌞 South-facing and positioned on a gentle hill, Villa Azura catches the sun all year — even in winter — and benefits from a refreshing coastal breeze in summer. Perfect for both summer holidays and longer winter stays.

Villa Azura is situated in the Los Almendros residential area of Benissa Costa — a tranquil neighbourhood on a gentle hillside. The surrounding Marina Alta region is regarded as one of the healthiest climates in the world, with more than 300 days of sunshine a year.

A landscape of sandy beaches, hidden coves, medieval villages, vineyards, nature reserves and rugged coastal paths awaits. Ask us for our personal restaurant tips!

Explore the Area

Things To Do Around Moraira & Benissa

The Costa Blanca has something for everyone — hidden coves, cliff walks, golf, markets and outstanding restaurants just minutes away.

Walking distance

Cala Baladrar

A tranquil pebbly bay with calm, crystal-clear water and a beach bar (Chiringuito Olalá). Perfect for a morning swim — the walk from the villa takes about 20 minutes along quiet streets.

📍 1,500 m from Villa Azura
Blue Flag

Moraira Beach & El Portet

Moraira has two sandy Blue Flag beaches. The main beach is lively with facilities; El Portet is sheltered with crystal-clear water — great for children and a favourite for evening strolls.

📍 5 min drive
Snorkelling spot

Cala l'Andrago & Cala Advocat

Beautiful rocky coves just down from the villa, popular with snorkellers and divers for rich marine life and turquoise water. Accessible by car with limited parking.

📍 ~2 km
Lively resort

Calpe & La Fossa Beach

A long sandy beach in the shadow of the iconic Peñón de Ifach rock. Calpe's old town and harbour are worth exploring, with a fresh fish market on Tuesday and Friday mornings.

📍 ~10 min drive
Iconic hike

Cap d'Or Watchtower

A 30-minute walk from El Portet beach up to a historic watchtower with one of the most breathtaking panoramas on the entire Costa Blanca — on a clear day you can see as far as Ibiza.

📍 El Portet, Moraira
Cliff walk

Paseo Ecológico Benissa

A stunning coastal trail running along the cliffs of Benissa Costa — right near the villa. Dramatic sea views, wild flowers and fresh air. One of the area's most popular walks.

📍 Benissa Costa, near villa
Dramatic scenery

Peñón de Ifach, Calpe

The iconic 332m sea rock that defines the Costa Blanca skyline. Hike to the top through a tunnel in the rock for extraordinary views. Allow 2–3 hours return.

📍 ~10 min drive
Cycling & water sports

Cycling & Water Sports

Cycle to Calpe with views of the Ifach rock or inland toward Llíber. Moraira harbour offers kayak rentals, stand-up paddleboarding, diving courses and dolphin-watching cruises.

📍 Moraira harbour & surroundings
2 km from villa

Golf Ifach

A scenic 9-hole course with sea views just minutes from Villa Azura. Popular with all levels, with a good clubhouse restaurant. One of the best-located courses on the Costa Blanca.

📍 ~2 km from Villa Azura
18 holes

Club de Golf Jávea

An 18-hole course set among orange groves and almond trees near Jávea, with a beautiful mountain backdrop and welcoming clubhouse.

📍 ~20 min drive
18 holes

Villaitana Golf

Two 18-hole courses near Benidorm — ideal for a full golfing day trip. The Poniente course is particularly scenic with views towards the Puig Campana mountain.

📍 ~30 min drive
Every Friday

Moraira Friday Market

Every Friday morning the centre of Moraira transforms into a colourful local market with fresh produce, olives, cheeses, local wines, leather bags, clothing and handmade ceramics.

📍 Moraira centre, 5 min drive
Wine & almonds

Jalón/Xaló Valley & Wineries

The Jalón valley is famous for its muscatel wine and almond orchards. Visit the Sunday market and the co-operative winery for local wines at unbeatable prices. A lovely half-day excursion.

📍 ~20 min drive
June / July

Moros y Cristianos Festival

Held each June/July in Moraira, this spectacular fiesta features colourful parades, traditional costumes and fireworks celebrating the Christian reconquest of Spain. Not to be missed!

📍 Moraira
10 min

Calpe Old Town

The charming old town of Calpe sits below the Peñón de Ifach with whitewashed streets, a fish market (Tue & Fri), tapas bars and a beautiful Gothic church.

📍 ~10 min drive
White village

Altea

One of the most beautiful villages on the Costa Blanca — a labyrinth of whitewashed streets, blue-domed church, art galleries and excellent fish restaurants on the seafront promenade.

📍 ~25 min drive
Mountain castle

Guadalest

A spectacular medieval castle village perched on top of a mountain, accessible through a tunnel carved in the rock. The reservoir views are stunning. A must-see day trip.

📍 ~45 min drive
City break

Alicante & Valencia

Alicante offers a stunning castle, beautiful esplanade and a great tapas scene (~70 km). Valencia — home of paella — has the unmissable City of Arts & Sciences and a vibrant old town (~100 km).

📍 1–1.5 hr drive
Beach Bar · Cala Baladrar

Chiringuito Olalá

A lovely beach bar right at Cala Baladrar — your nearest beach from the villa. Great for a relaxed lunch or evening drink with your feet in the sand, virtually on your doorstep.

💶 Low–Mid · Cala Baladrar
Fine Dining · El Portet

Le Dauphin

Probably the best-situated restaurant on the Costa Blanca, overlooking El Portet beach. Swiss owners with a Michelin kitchen recommendation. Dine on the summer terrace with sea views and a gentle breeze.

💶 High · Tel: +34 96 649 0432
Paella & Seafood · Beachfront

El Chamizo

Right on the beach just outside Moraira — romantic for dinner or a relaxed lunch while the kids play on the sand. Known for outstanding paella (pre-order at busy times).

💶 Mid · Tel: +34 965 743 090
Creative Tapas · Moraira

Tasca 42

Innovative tapas made with fresh local ingredients, with vegetarian, vegan and gluten-free options. A favourite with locals and visitors alike on the coastal road toward Calpe.

💶 Mid · Tel: +34 722 310 923
Authentic Spanish · Moraira centre

El Rincón de Moraira

Family-run for over 30 years by Ramón and his two chef children. Extensive menu, everything fresh daily and a delicious traditional paella. A true local institution.

💶 Mid · Tel: +34 966 490 360
Fusion · On the Golf Course

Kosta d'Oro

Chic restaurant right on Golf Ifach — perfect after a round. Mediterranean-Italian-Japanese fusion kitchen with a wine cellar of over 2,000 bottles.

💶 Mid-High · Tel: +34 965 212 713
French Country · Jalón Valley

Verd i Vent

Extraordinary value in a rustic, atmospheric setting in the Jalón valley. Four-course set meal with local wine from the barrel for around €15. Book essential.

💶 Low · Tel: +34 965 973 413
